Keywords

How It Works

Companies send targeted ads based on certain keywords from your digital profile. By consistently searching random words, algorithms will not have accurate profiles and won't be able to show ads that will influence you directly.

Your Data

In addition to tracking searches, companies also look at the browser you use, the device you are using, and more. The following types of data are collected:

  • Personal Data
  • Engagement Data
  • Behavioral Data
  • Attitudinal Data
